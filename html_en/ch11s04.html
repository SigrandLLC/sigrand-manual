Interaction of SNMPD with EOCD

SNMPD receives the SHDSL-channel status data from the daemon EOCD . Therefore, in order to receive data conserning the state of the SHDSL-channel via SNMP, the EOCD daemon should be adjusted. After the data is available via the eoc-info utility — the data can be received via SNMP. The detailed information about the functionality and adjustment of the EOCD daemon is given in chapter SHDSL-channels monitoring .

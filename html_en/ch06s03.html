Bonding of interfaces states

To create complex interconnections between interfaces there was added an option of managing the states of the Ethernet or E1 port depending on the state of the correspondent SHDSL port.

Figure 6.2. Bonding of interfaces states


It is fixed under the given configuration that in case of link drop-out at dsl0 interface, the link at the interfaces eth3 and E1_0 will be subject to a despotic shutdown.

Note

SHDSL-interfaces may function as link-master, Ethernet and E1 - as link-slave.

Abstract

This Manual concernes such aspects as adjustment and operation of the routers of the 17th series manufactured by LLC SIGRAND ( SG-17R).

A brief description of characteristics, options, operational conditions, network interfaces settings and supported modules of the router is given.

Network interfaces and services adjustement procedures, and different operational modes of the router (multiplexing, bridging, bonding, VLAN, QoS, VoIP and others) are explained.

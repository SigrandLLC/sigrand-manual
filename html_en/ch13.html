Chapter 13. Warranty and liabilities

The Manufacturer guarantees the operability of the router under the condition that the user obliges the operating rules.

Warranty period - 5 years from the date of purchase indicated in the certificate, or, in the absence thereof, from the date of manufacturing indicated on the label.

Warning

Warranty is cancelled for routers used in overhead lines or in lines with a combined method of construction.

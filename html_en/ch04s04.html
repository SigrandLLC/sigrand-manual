Expert mode

In expert mode you can update the loader of the router and carry out some actions with the flash memory of the router.

Important

Use expert mode only if you are aware of your actions! These actions may lead to disability of the router!

Actuation of expert mode is carried out in configuration menu of the loader. Expert password - adm5120.

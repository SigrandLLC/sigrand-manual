Phone book

The phonebook is located at the page Hardware/VoIP/Phone book. It is used for speed-dial of telephone numbers. For example, for the number 100 02 833 the short number 10 can be set, and #10 can be used for dialling.

Figure 10.9. Phone book


Addition of a new record to the table is shown in the figure below.

Figure 10.10. Phone book: addition of a new record


  • Enabled — the record is active or not. If the record is not active, it will not be added to the configuration file.

  • Short number — two-digit short number.

  • Complete number — full number addressed when dialling "#short_number". When indicating the full number, the "_" symbol can be used to divide groupd of digits. Here a SIP-number can be indicated in the fromat: #sip:user1@domain#.

  • Comment — comment to the record.

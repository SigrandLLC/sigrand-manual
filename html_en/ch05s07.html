Utilities

On page System/tools ofweb-interface various utilities are available which are used for the device performance analysis or functional check.

  • syslog — grant access to the logs obtained from "logread" utility;

  • dmesg — grant access to the logs obtained from "dmesg" utility;

  • ping — allows checking the availability of the site using icmp protocol;

  • mtr — allows checking the quality of connection between the device and the remote site.
